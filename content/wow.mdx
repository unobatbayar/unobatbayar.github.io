### My Childhood Game

*World of Warcraft* (WoW) has always held a special place in my heart. I started playing back in 2010, and like many players, I was completely captivated by its vast world, epic lore, and the thrill of exploring Azeroth with friends. One of the most memorable expansions for me was *Wrath of the Lich King* — an experience I’ll never forget.

#### Wrath of the Lich King: A Legendary Expansion

The release of *Wrath of the Lich King* marked a turning point in WoW's history. The expansion's dark and immersive storyline, featuring the terrifying Lich King, was both haunting and exciting. The atmosphere in Northrend was unlike anything I had seen in a game before — from the icy peaks of Icecrown Citadel to the eerie music that played as you ventured deeper into the land. I spent countless hours questing, raiding, and trying to level up my character as I got lost in this epic narrative.

#### My Favorite Class: Rogue

My class of choice in WoW was always the **Rogue**. There's something exhilarating about sneaking through the shadows, backstabbing enemies, and executing perfectly timed combos. The Rogue’s agility, stealth mechanics, and ability to take down foes from the shadows have always felt incredibly satisfying. Whether I was in PvE raids or PvP battlegrounds, playing as a Rogue always made me feel like a true master of stealth.

Rogues have that unique ability to control the flow of combat. From picking pockets in Dalaran to laying waste to enemies in battlegrounds, the versatility of the Rogue class kept me coming back for more.

#### Memorable Tracks and Themes

Some of WoW’s most beloved tracks have become part of the game’s identity. Here are a few that stand out:

- "Lament of the Highborne" – The haunting song sung by the blood elves in Silvermoon captures the tragic beauty of their history. It’s an emotional ballad that has remained a fan favorite since its debut.

- "A Call to Arms" – This theme, first heard in the cinematic trailer for The Burning Crusade, is a powerful rallying call to arms for the Alliance, full of determination and hope. It captures the spirit of adventuring and fighting for a cause that unites players.

- "Invincible" – The music accompanying The Lich King's final moments is chilling and deeply evocative. The sorrow and finality of the piece underscore the tragic nature of the character, bringing a heightened emotional weight to the raid encounter.

- "Warlords of Draenor: Main Theme" – The powerful, driving rhythm of this theme perfectly reflects the grand conflict of Warlords of Draenor, a battle between alternate realities and the strength of the Horde and Alliance. It’s aggressive yet stirring, a fitting anthem for the expansion.

- "The Shattering" – From the Cataclysm expansion, this piece is deeply atmospheric and tense, capturing the chaotic transformation of Azeroth. It’s a haunting reminder of the world-changing events that shaped the game.

#### Addons

World of Warcraft (WoW) addons are third-party modifications that players can install to enhance their gameplay experience. These addons are written using the Lua programming language and XML, and they can alter nearly any aspect of the game’s user interface (UI), from the way the game looks to how it functions. Addons allow players to customize their UI, automate certain tasks, improve accessibility, and add new features to the game.

My favorite addons were SexyMap and Bartender4; nowadays, they can be easily installed via CurseForge.

#### Why WoW Still Stands the Test of Time

Even though I don't play WoW as much as I used to, it remains a nostalgic part of my childhood. The friendships I made, the excitement of leveling up, and the sense of accomplishment from defeating bosses like the Lich King himself are memories I will cherish forever. WoW was more than just a game; it was an experience that shaped my love for storytelling, strategy, and community in gaming.
